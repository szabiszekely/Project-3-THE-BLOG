My time in the Budai Castle

Post by Székely Szabolcs, Feburay 26, 2023.
Travel Story

Last week me and my family went to the Budai Castle for a tour. First we picked up my dad from his work place. After that we took the bus, because as my mother once said "I'm not gonna climb that mountain!". We talked a lot and took a few pictures as well. We ate the sweets that my mother brought with her than I started to annoy my little brother. However in the process he fell down one of the stony stairs and hurt his ancles. Mom got really angry at me so much that I wasn't able to talk to her on the rest of the trip. When we arrived at home I made the dinner and went to take a bath. When I got out of the tub the entire family sit around the table and played UNO. I quckly dressed up and started playing with them. When we finished I apolagized to my mom, and my mom started renting why it was so dangerous. In the end no body got hurt exept the little bro.
